Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dot Diary collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Dot Diary website and app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Account information, including your email address.
  • Diary data you create in the app, such as dot types, entries, note text, settings, and related metadata.
  • Basic technical data needed to provide the service, like authentication/session information.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and secure Dot Diary.
  • Authenticate your account and let you sign in with magic links.
  • Sync your diary data across devices.
  • Troubleshoot service issues and prevent abuse.

3. Storage and Processing

Dot Diary uses Supabase to provide authentication and cloud data storage. Data may be processed and stored by this provider on our behalf.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Dot Diary uses essential storage/session technologies required to keep you signed in and operate core app functionality. We do not use non-essential advertising cookies in the current version of the app.

5. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers that help operate Dot Diary (for example, infrastructure and auth providers).
  • When required by law or legal process.
  • To protect rights, safety, and security of users and the service.

6. Data Retention

We retain account and diary data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You can request account and data deletion by contacting us.

7. Your Privacy Rights
